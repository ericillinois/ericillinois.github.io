US Covid Cases and Death During Pandemic

Zhao Li

The Covid-19 Narrative Visualization on United States displays the accumulative New Cases and Death of Covid-19 cases in US 2020-01-20 to 2023-03-22 , we can see the trend of the cases and death trend in US very clearly.

We have five buttons at the top of the page. The first button is Three Waves For New Cases Chart, which showcases the three distinct waves of new cases. The second button, Explore New Cases By Time Chart, lets you delve into new cases over time. The third button is titled Three Waves For Death Chart and highlights the three prominent waves of deaths. The fourth button is Explore New Deaths By Time Chart, designed to provide insights into the death due to Covid-19 over time. Finally, the fifth button is the New Cases and Death Chart by States, which allows you to explore the cumulative cases and deaths in each state.
